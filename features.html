01 — Scanning

How a photo becomes a wardrobe item.

Every scan runs the same five steps, whether you're a guest just trying it out or logged into your account.

1. Upload Photo

Take or upload a photo of your item.

2. Teachable Machine

Our model built in Google Teachable Machine analyzes the image.

3. Category Prediction

The algorithm predicts the most likely category.

4. Review & Edit

You can confirm or change the category.

5. Save to Wardrobe

Add the item to your wardrobe if you're logged in.

Logged in Choose "Add to wardrobe" to save the item, or keep scanning more pieces before you stop.
Not logged in? Continue scanning or create an account to save your items.

02 — Access

Guest, registered, or verified.

What you can do depends on where your account stands:every limit lifts once your email is confirmed.

Guest

No account
  • Scan clothes & get AI recognition
  • Browse the landing page & features
  • Read about, reviews & contact us
Cannot save any items: nothing is kept after the session.

Registered

Email not confirmed
  • Save clothes to your wardrobe
  • Everything is capped until you confirm
Limit: 2 items per category · 2 favorites · 2 planned outfits. The limit can't be exceeded until confirmation.
Check your inbox to unlock the rest.

Verified account

Email confirmed
  • Unlimited wardrobe & favorites
  • Unlimited outfit planning & calendar
  • Full archives & statistics
  • Everything unlocked, no limits
