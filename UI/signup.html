Terms of Service & Specifications

This document outlines how Pick Your Fit collects, processes, stores, and secures user data and media, ensuring compliance with global privacy regulations (including GDPR and CCPA).

1. Personal Data Collection & Purpose

We collect minimal personal data to ensure the platform operates securely and provides a personalized experience:

Standard Identifiers:

  • First & Last Name: Collected during registration (firstName, lastName) to personalize the user interface and account settings.
  • Email Address: Used as a unique account identifier, for essential service communications, account recovery, and identity verification.

Technical & Authentication Data:

  • Password: Collected at sign-up and secured using industry-standard, salted hashing algorithms managed by Firebase Authentication. The platform cannot read or access your plain-text password.
  • IP Address: Temporarily logged during account creation to enforce security measures and prevent platform abuse (strictly enforcing a limit of 2 accounts per IP address).

2. Image Processing & Storage (Media Handling)

Pick Your Fit processes two categories of user-uploaded images. Both are securely tied to your unique user ID (uid) and are never made public without your consent:

Profile Picture (Avatar):

  • Processing: Users can upload a photo which is cropped and optimized directly on the client side using Cropper.js to a fixed resolution of 512x512 pixels (JPEG format, compressed at 88% quality) to minimize bandwidth.
  • Storage: The finalized image is stored in our secure cloud media repository on Cloudinary. The secure URL (photoUrl) is then saved to the user's Firestore profile document.

Wardrobe Photos (Closet Items):

  • Background Removal: When scanning a clothing item, the image is securely routed via our backend proxy to the remove.bg (Kaleido AI) API to isolate the clothing item. This processing is ephemeral; the third-party service does not retain your image.
  • Storage: The processed, background-free image is uploaded to a dedicated user folder on Cloudinary (wardrobe/{uid}). The secure HTTPS link is stored in the user's Firestore wardrobe sub-collection along with metadata (e.g., brand, category, description, and createdAt timestamp).

3. Data Infrastructure & Storage Locations

To keep your data safe, we distribute storage across specialized, secure environments:

  • Cloud Database (Google Firebase Firestore): All structured user profile metadata, wardrobe records, and consent timestamps are stored on Google Firebase’s secure Cloud Firestore. Access is strictly governed by Firestore Security Rules, ensuring users can only read, write, or delete their own data. Security is enforced via JSON Web Tokens (JWT) passed as a Bearer token in authorization headers.
  • Media Cloud (Cloudinary): Physical image files (avatars and wardrobe items) are hosted on Cloudinary and delivered via highly secure, encrypted HTTPS Content Delivery Networks (CDNs).
  • Local Storage (Browser LocalStorage): To maintain an active session without requiring you to log in repeatedly, we store the following variables locally in your browser: pyf_idToken, pyf_uid, and pyf_userPhoto. These are strictly functional storage keys required for system operation; we do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

4. Third-Party Data Processors

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share data with trusted third-party sub-processors necessary to run the application:

  • Google Firebase: Manages secure user authentication and hosts our structured database.
  • Cloudinary: Handles cloud storage, optimization, and delivery of your images.
  • remove.bg (Kaleido AI): Temporarily processes wardrobe uploads to strip backgrounds from clothing items.

5. User Rights & The "Cascade Delete" Process

In compliance with the GDPR "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17), users have absolute control over their data. Triggering the account deletion process from the Account Management panel executes an automated, permanent Cascade Delete:

  • Wardrobe Purge: Every document, category, and entry in the user's Firestore wardrobe sub-collection is permanently deleted.
  • Cloud Asset Cleanup: The system calls Cloudinary’s API (deleteAsset) to physically and permanently destroy the user's avatar and all images stored in their wardrobe/{uid} folder.
  • Profile Deletion: The user's primary Firestore profile document is wiped out.
  • Auth Account Removal: The login credentials (email and hashed password) are deleted from Firebase Authentication.
  • Local Wipe: The browser's LocalStorage is cleared immediately, terminating the active session.
⚠️ Critical Warning: This deletion process is completely irreversible. We do not keep offline backups or archive deleted user accounts. Once deleted, your wardrobe and profile are gone forever.

Failure to comply will result in immediate, permanent account termination and, where required by law, cooperation with local authorities.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Pick Your Fit. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it, in compliance with major data protection regulations such as the GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the platform. The data we collect is strictly limited to what is necessary for the app to function:

  • Account Information: Your first name, last name, and email address.
  • Authentication Data: A hashed version of your password (managed securely by Google Firebase) and your IP address (logged temporarily during sign-up to enforce our security limit of 2 accounts per IP).
  • Media and Images: Photos you upload for your profile picture (avatar) and images of clothing items you scan to add to your virtual wardrobe.
  • Local Session Data: To keep you logged in, we store specific functional identifiers in your browser's LocalStorage (pyf_idToken, pyf_uid, pyf_userPhoto). We do not use tracking cookies or advertising trackers.

2. How We Process and Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to provide, operate, and maintain our application:

  • Account Management: To create and secure your account, and personalize your user dashboard.
  • Avatar Processing: Profile pictures are cropped and optimized directly on your device before being uploaded.
  • Wardrobe Processing: When you scan a clothing item, the image is temporarily sent through a secure proxy to a third-party service (remove.bg / Kaleido AI) to remove the background. This processing is ephemeral, and the third party does not store your images.
  • Service Delivery: To load and display your digital closet across your devices securely.

3. Where Your Data is Stored

To ensure high security and reliability, your data is distributed across trusted, industry-leading cloud providers:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore & Authentication): Your text-based profile metadata, wardrobe item details (brand, category, timestamp), and secure login credentials are encrypted and stored here.
  • Cloudinary: All physical image files (your cropped avatar and background-removed wardrobe photos) are hosted on Cloudinary's secure servers and delivered via an encrypted HTTPS connection.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share information with the following trusted third-party service providers (sub-processors) specifically to run our application:

  • Google (Firebase): For database hosting and user authentication.
  • Cloudinary: For secure image hosting and delivery.
  • Kaleido AI (remove.bg): Strictly for the temporary processing of clothing images to remove backgrounds.

5. Data Retention and Account Deletion (Cascade Delete)

You have absolute control over your data. We retain your personal information only for as long as your account is active.

If you choose to delete your account via the "Account Management" settings, our system immediately triggers a permanent Cascade Delete process:

  • All wardrobe items and metadata are erased from our Firestore database.
  • All images (avatars and closet photos) associated with your account are permanently deleted from Cloudinary's servers.
  • Your profile document and authentication credentials are completely removed.
  • Your browser's local session data is cleared.
Warning: Because we respect your right to be forgotten, this process is completely irreversible and we do not maintain backups of deleted accounts.

6. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children. You must be at least 13 years of age (or 16 years of age depending on your local jurisdiction, such as the EEA) to use our services. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover a minor has created an account, we will immediately delete their information.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., under the GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate data.
  • Request the complete erasure of your data (Right to be Forgotten) using our automated in-app deletion tool.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time (which is done by deleting your account).
